Raster to Vector Conversion - Experimental Results

You need to install the Adobe SVG Viewer plugin to display the SVG graphics (use Internet Explorer).

If you get a blurred image, right-click on the image and disable the Higher Quality setting of the plugin.

The outputs have been generated by the commands: 

spvec.exe polygon.png polygon.1.svg svg_lines1=1
spvec.exe polygon.png polygon.2.svg svg_curves=1 svg_control=1
spvec.exe a.png a.svg svg_curves=1
spvec.exe b.png b.svg svg_curves=1
spvec.exe c.png c.svg svg_curves=1
spvec.exe d.png d.svg svg_curves=1
spvec.exe e.png e.svg svg_curves=1
spvec.exe f.png f.svg svg_curves=1
